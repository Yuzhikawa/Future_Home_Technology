Smart Lamp

Lamps have the ability to adjust the light automatically or through intelligent adjustments, such as using a motion sensor or timing.

Home Automation

Home Automation allows home devices and systems to connect to each other via a network, enabling remote control and exchange of information between devices.

Smart Security

Smart Security uses technology and artificial intelligence to protect your home or property in smart ways, such as facial recognition or advanced motion detection.

Smart Switch

Smart Switch has the ability to intelligently control the flow of electricity to connected devices or lights, either through a smartphone app, voice, or a predetermined schedule.

Smart Speaker

Smart Speakers have artificial intelligence that allows users to interact with voice and perform various tasks, such as playing music, providing information, answering questions, and controlling other devices.

Smart Temp Control

Smart Temperature Control can connect to WiFi networks and other devices, such as smartphones or smart home systems, allowing users to control room temperature remotely and access settings via an app.

Smart Tv

Smart TVs are equipped with an intelligent operating system and can connect to the internet, allowing users to access applications, streaming content and online services.
